Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how LinaBerg ("we", "us") handles information in connection with our content platform and TikTok integration.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our services, including:

  • TikTok account data required for content posting (username, access tokens)
  • Content metadata (video titles, descriptions, posting schedules)

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to automate content posting to TikTok on behalf of the authorized account owner. We do not sell, share, or use data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and credentials are stored locally on the operator's device and are never transmitted to third parties other than TikTok's official API.

4. Third-Party Services

We use TikTok's Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's own Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain access tokens only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
